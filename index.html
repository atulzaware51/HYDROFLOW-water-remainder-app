Features

Everything you need to
stay in flow

Built around your daily rhythm — HydroFlow gives you the tools and nudges to make hydration second nature.

Smart Progress Tracking
A live progress ring and cup tracker show your hydration at a glance. Watch your stats update in real time as you log each drink — glasses, millilitres, and percentage all visible instantly.
Core Feature
Reminder Timer
Set custom intervals from 15 to 90 minutes. A live countdown reminds you to hydrate with a gentle chime.
Streaks & Achievements
Unlock 6 badges and track your daily streak. Confetti fires every time you reach your goal.
Drink History Log
Every glass is timestamped and saved. Review your hydration timeline and clear it anytime.
Custom Goal Setting
Set your own daily glass count and ml per glass. The whole app adapts to your personal hydration goal.
Dark & Light Modes
Switch between a deep ocean dark theme and a crisp light mode. Your preference is saved automatically.
How It Works

Three steps to
daily hydration

1
Set Your Goal
Choose how many glasses and millilitres per day suit your body and lifestyle. Fully customisable.
2
Log Every Drink
Tap "Log a Drink" each time you hydrate. Watch the ring fill, cups light up, and your stats climb.
3
Build Your Streak
Hit your goal each day to extend your streak. Unlock achievements and develop a lasting habit.
